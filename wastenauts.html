Wastenauts

Case Study

In my senior capstone course, we worked on a team to create a new business venture. This entailed putting together everything but a finished product or service (customer validation, market strategy, financials, minimum viable product) and then presenting to a panel of judges. At the time of this project, I put together a very basic representation of our product (a mobile application) for our minimum viable product, but I have since put together a more fleshed out comprehensive prototype.

My Role

UX Strategy, User Research, Branding, UI Design

Tools Used

Adobe Illustrator, Sketch, InVision

Problem

Design a mobile application that features a simple and seamless way to track grocery inventory and a direct channel for food manufacturing companies to market to their consumers.

Solution

My solution is a mobile application called Wastenauts.

View Prototype

Target User

The target user is a young professional. These are people who are 20-34 years old who are only buying food for themselves. They are inexperienced shoppers who are busy with their career. They are not organized when it comes to keeping track of their fridge and pantry contents. On average, a consumer under the age of 30 wastes $500 per year due to food spoilage.
