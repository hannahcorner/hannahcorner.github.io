Handy

Case Study

Homes don’t come with an owners manual. Handy allows homeowners to navigate the uncertainty and complexity of home maintenance.

My Role

UX Strategy, User Research, Branding, UI Design

Tools Used

Adobe Illustrator, Figma, Google Forms

Problem

It is difficult for homeowners to manage home maintenance tasks. Knowing what needs to be done and remembering to do it can be challenging.

Solution

A home maintenance task management app called Handy. Handy allows users to create tasks, keep notes about their house, and look up local home maintenance professionals.

View Prototype

User research

Personas

I developed three personas for this project. You can think of them as the novice homeowner, the experienced homeowner, and the middle-of-the-road homeowner. They have different knowledge levels when it comes to home maintenance which effects their goals and motivations.
